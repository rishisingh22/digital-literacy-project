#  Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown payment requests  
- Verify links before clicking  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions  
- Keep your apps and devices updated  
- Be cautious of calls claiming to be from banks  

##  UPI Safety Tips
- Always check the receiver’s name before sending money  
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money  

##  Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
